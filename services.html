Corporate Management & Strategic Planning

The starting point for most TargetPath consulting engagements is a comprehensive creation or review of the client's strategic plan to ensure it delivers the high-level business assumptions, value proposition, competitive advantage and business model around which the business is centered to ensure optimum success.

Beginning with market research data, TargetPath creates a compelling and comprehensive product positioning, messaging, marketing communications and branding plan. Encompassing everything from PR to collateral, web development, logo design, events, advertising and various social techniques and digital media plans, we have the talent to create all types of effective marketing communications tools and a strategy to support your goals and budget.

Product Innovation

The formulation of a competitive product roadmap, along with the technologies and differentiating features needed to deliver a protectable market position are the primary objectives of the product strategy. TargetPath can then work with suppliers to deliver the product as well as create a detailed marketing launch plan and assist in its flawless execution.
