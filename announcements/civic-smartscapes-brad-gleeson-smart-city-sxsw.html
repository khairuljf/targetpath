CIVIQ Smartscapes' Brad Gleeson to speak at Smart City Day at SXSW

10-Mar-2017 
 
Brad Gleeson, Chief Commercial Officer, CIVIQ Smartscapes will be adding CIVIQ's perspective on what's working, what's needed and what is to come at the Smart City Day at SXSW. The session is this Sunday March 12th at the San Antonio House in Austin, and will run from 11am - 4pm. Brad will be speaking at 11:55am onwards. 

 


Session Description: 

Connected technologies are transforming cities. Through a series of lightning talks (no boring panels allowed), mayors, city technology officers and entrepreneurs will showcase what they are doing, what works, what is needed and what is to come. You'll hear about how top tech trends - the Internet of Things (IoT), big data, autonomous vehicles, connected buildings and homes, smart grids and more are impacting the most pressing challenges for cities - mobility, affordability, energy, sustainability, access to healthcare and education, citizen engagement, the digital divide and more. Join us, meet smart city leaders from around the world, learn, share and participate in building the cities of the future.
